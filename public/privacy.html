Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use AI Video Automation, we collect:

  • TikTok account information (username, profile) via OAuth authorization
  • YouTube account information via OAuth authorization
  • Content you generate through the Service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate and connect to your TikTok and YouTube accounts
  • Generate and publish video content on your behalf
  • Improve the Service

3. Data Storage

Your access tokens are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your tokens to external servers beyond the necessary platform API calls.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the Service (i.e., publishing content to connected platforms).

5. Content

All video content is generated by AI based on publicly available trending topics. The Service does not collect personal data from individuals mentioned in trending topics.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your account credentials. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You can disconnect your TikTok or YouTube accounts at any time through the Service dashboard. This will revoke the Service's access to your accounts.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the application dashboard.
